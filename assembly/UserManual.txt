version 0.1.0
myOS now supports several commands:
1. mkf-make a file. You need to specify head, sector, and cylinder in which you want to store, then you can type and press enter to save.
2. open-open a file. You need to specify head, sector, and cylinder in which your file stored.
3. echo-echo what you just typed
4. clear-clear screen
Warning: mkf and open commands can only offically suport sector(cylinder, head) from 1 to 9.

installation:
Creating a virtual machine and setting myOS.img as its floppy.
